# Iris

## Basic Info
- **Full Name**: Iris
- **Age**: Around 10-11 (same as Alex)
- **Species**: Wonderland native
- **Role**: Alex's best friend, guide, emotional anchor
- **Lives in**: Garden District

## Physical Description
- Born of Wonderland itself—appearance reflects the Garden District
- Skin has a subtle iridescent quality, like flower petals in sunlight
- Hair changes color slightly with emotions (soft pastels—greens, blues, purples)
- Eyes are bright and expressive, color of morning glories
- Dressed in clothes that seem to be woven from living plants (vines, leaves, soft blooms)
- Moves with natural grace, comfortable in Wonderland's fluid physics
- About Alex's height, slender build

## Personality
- **Empathetic**: Can "read" emotions in plants and people
- **Patient teacher**: Helps Alex learn to see Wonderland properly
- **Curious about the above world**: Never been there, fascinated by Alex's stories
- **Brave but cautious**: Willing to face danger for friends, but thoughtful about it
- **Genuinely kind**: No hidden agenda, just wants to help and connect
- **Playful**: Enjoys Wonderland's impossibilities, teaches Alex to have fun
- **Loyal**: Stands by Alex even when it's scary

## Background
- Born in Wonderland, never known another world
- Grew up in the Garden District, learned to communicate with plants
- Has seen other visitors come and go, knows they always leave eventually
- Parents/family: Unclear—Wonderland natives may not have traditional families
- Learned from the Garden Tender about nurturing and growth
- Lonely in her own way—visitors are temporary, other natives are very different from her

## Character Arc
**Beginning**:
- Meets Alex as another temporary visitor
- Expects him to leave like all the others
- Guards her heart a little, knowing friendship might be brief

**Middle**:
- Opens up to Alex, allows herself to truly care
- Teaches him, learns from him
- Discovers that friendship matters even if it's temporary
- Risks herself to help save Wonderland

**End**:
- Accepts that Alex will leave but their friendship is real and permanent
- Promises to wait, to remember, to keep their connection alive
- Grows more confident in her own worth and importance to Wonderland

## Relationships

**Alex**: Best friend, first genuine connection with a visitor who truly saw her
**Garden Tender**: Mentor figure who taught her about growth and patience
**Bell Keeper**: Respects as elder/authority but finds intimidating
**Wonderland itself**: Deep connection, can feel its moods and health

## Special Abilities
- Can "read" emotions in plants and gardens
- Wonderland responds more readily to her (native advantage)
- Teaches Alex to see with his heart instead of eyes
- Can sense when Wonderland is in distress

## Role in Story
- **Guide**: Teaches Alex how Wonderland works
- **Heart**: Represents the belonging Alex seeks
- **Mirror**: Her loneliness reflects his—different world, same need
- **Catalyst**: Her friendship enables Alex's transformation
- **Stake**: Her safety and happiness motivate Alex's sacrifice

## Key Moments
- Chapter 6: Meets Alex, teaches him to "see" Wonderland
- Chapter 7: Garden adventure, they become true friends
- Chapter 10: Finds Alex in Mirror District, supports him
- Chapter 12: Helps Alex during crisis, shows bravery
- Chapter 14: Gives Alex her friendship as a gift/strength
- Chapter 15: Tells Alex she won't blame him if he leaves
- Chapter 19: Tearful goodbye, promise to remember
- (Implied) Chapter 20: Waits for Alex's return

## Character Voice
- Speaks with wonder about ordinary things from Alex's world
- Uses nature metaphors naturally
- Gentle but direct—doesn't hide truth to spare feelings
- Asks curious questions, genuinely listens to answers
- Optimistic but not naive

## Notes & Development
- Name "Iris" works on multiple levels: flower, eye (seeing), rainbow bridge between worlds
- She's not a sidekick—she's Alex's equal with her own arc
- Her loneliness is different from Alex's but just as real
- Consider: Does she appear in future books if this becomes a series?
