# Shimmer (The Hummingbird Creature)

## Basic Info
- **Full Name**: Shimmer
- **Age**: Ancient (first visitor to Wonderland, chose to stay)
- **Species**: Former human, now Wonderland native
- **Role**: Guide, mentor, mysterious helper
- **Form**: Hummingbird-humanoid hybrid

## Physical Description
- Size: Tiny—face no bigger than a thumb
- Body: Mix of hummingbird and small humanoid
- Wings: Iridescent, blur with motion, create sparkles of light when flying
- Face: Surprisingly human, delicate features, ancient eyes
- Colors: Shift between blues, greens, purples, like oil on water
- Trails sparkles of light when moving, like a tiny comet
- Hovers at eye level when talking to people
- Voice: Like wind chimes, musical but clear

## Personality
- **Mysterious**: Knows more than revealed, speaks in hints and questions
- **Playful**: Despite age, maintains childlike wonder and mischief
- **Wise**: Understands Wonderland's nature deeply
- **Compassionate**: Genuinely cares about visitors, especially lonely ones
- **Patient**: Has seen many visitors, knows transformation takes time
- **Slightly cryptic**: Answers questions with questions, teaches through discovery
- **Dual nature**: Can be lighthearted or serious depending on need

## Background
- **Secret**: Was the first human visitor to Wonderland, centuries ago
- Came as a child, lonely and seeking wonder
- Fell so in love with Wonderland that when offered the door home, chose to stay
- Transformation: Became part of Wonderland itself, took form of hummingbird-person
- Has guided many visitors since, always from the shadows
- Remembers the human world but as a distant dream
- Witnessed Wonderland's wound forming, has waited for someone who could heal it

## Character Arc
**Beginning**:
- Mysterious guide who appears at key moments
- Offers cryptic advice, seems to enjoy Alex's confusion
- Identity as former human is secret

**Middle**:
- Becomes more serious as crisis develops
- Provides more direct guidance while maintaining mystery
- Bonds with Alex over shared experience

**End**:
- Reveals true identity—first visitor who stayed
- Shows Alex that both choices (staying/leaving) are valid
- Passes torch as "one who healed Wonderland" to Alex

## Relationships

**Alex**: Sees himself in Alex—lonely child seeking wonder
**Wonderland**: Completely integrated, part of its essence now
**Bell Keeper**: Old friends, co-guardians of Wonderland
**Iris**: Watches over her, knew her loneliness needed Alex
**All visitors**: Guides each one, remembers them all

## Special Abilities
- Can appear and disappear at will
- Senses visitors arriving before they land
- Understands both human and Wonderland perspectives
- Can communicate with all of Wonderland's inhabitants
- Flight allows access to all districts quickly

## Role in Story
- **Herald**: First to greet Alex, introduces Wonderland
- **Mentor**: Guides without controlling, teaches through questions
- **Mirror**: Shows Alex what staying vs. leaving means
- **Bridge**: Connects human and Wonderland perspectives
- **Witness**: Has seen the pattern of visitors, knows Alex is special

## Key Moments
- Chapter 1: First encounter, hints Alex was "looking for something"
- Chapter 2: Guides Alex to market, introduces wonder
- Chapter 5: "You're not ready to go through" the door
- Chapter 11: Becomes serious, reveals Wonderland is in danger
- Chapter 14: Reveals identity as first visitor, offers perspective
- Chapter 15: "The door will wait if you survive"
- Throughout: Appears at crucial moments with exactly the right cryptic advice

## Character Voice
- Asks questions instead of giving answers
- Uses "we" when talking about Wonderland (part of it now)
- Playful teasing mixed with ancient wisdom
- "You say that now..." (implies knowing the future)
- Speaks in short, memorable phrases
- Laughs like wind chimes

## Backstory (Revealed in Chapter 14)
- Came to Wonderland as a lonely child from the above world
- Parents were distant, felt invisible in their own life
- Wonderland gave them belonging, purpose, wonder
- When door home appeared, chose to stay forever
- Transformation was gradual—became more Wonderland, less human
- No regrets, but understands the weight of that choice
- Wants Alex to choose freely, not out of fear or obligation

## Notes & Development
- Name "Shimmer" reflects iridescent quality and ephemeral nature
- Gender: Deliberately ambiguous—Wonderland natives transcend human categories
- Voice them with they/them pronouns
- Key tension: Are they helping or manipulating Alex?
- Resolution: Genuinely helpful, but believes in Alex finding his own path
- Represents the road not taken—what if Alex stayed?
